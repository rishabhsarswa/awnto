## ⚡ Detailed Electrical Engineering Syllabus
- **Electrical Circuits**: Network theorems, AC/DC analysis, resonance, transient response  
- **Electrical Machines**: Transformers, DC machines, induction motors, synchronous machines  
- **Power Systems**: Generation, transmission, distribution, load flow, fault analysis  
- **Control Systems**: Feedback, stability, root locus, frequency response  
- **Electronics & Communication**: Diodes, transistors, amplifiers, digital logic, microprocessors  
- **Power Electronics**: Converters, inverters, rectifiers, choppers  
- **Measurement & Instrumentation**: Transducers, bridges, error analysis, CRO  
- **Switchgear & Protection**: Relays, circuit breakers, protection schemes  
- **Electrical Materials**: Conductors, insulators, semiconductors, magnetic materials  
- **Field Theory**: Electrostatics, magnetostatics, Maxwell’s equations
